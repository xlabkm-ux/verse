# World Magic Rules

## Project

Working title: `Verse`

## Core Rule

After sunset, human shadows disappear within the city limits.

## Boundaries

- The effect begins at full sunset, not at dusk.
- The effect ends at sunrise.
- Shadows disappear only inside the old city border.
- Reflections remain normal.
- Artificial light does not restore a missing shadow.

## Social Consequences

- Citizens treat the loss of shadows as normal, but outsiders often panic.
- Crimes committed after sunset are difficult to investigate because witness descriptions become unreliable.
- Religious groups interpret the missing shadows as a sign of judgment.

## Forbidden Contradictions

- No character can physically use a shadow after sunset inside the city.
- No scene may claim that a lantern or electric light brings the shadow back.
- A character outside the old city border keeps their shadow normally.
