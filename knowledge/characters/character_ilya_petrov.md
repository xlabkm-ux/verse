# Character Profile: Ilya Petrov

## Role

Ilya Petrov is Anna's younger brother.

## Age

Twenty-seven.

## Biography Facts

- He left the city at nineteen and returned only three days before his father's funeral.
- He owes money to people outside the city.
- He uses jokes and charm to deflect blame.
- He was absent for most of their father's illness.

## Personality

- impulsive
- defensive
- charismatic
- ashamed beneath the surface

## Canon Constraints

- Ilya masks guilt with irony.
- He provokes conflict when cornered.
- He was present at the funeral but arrived late.
