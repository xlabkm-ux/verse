# Character Profile: Anna Petrovna

## Role

Anna Petrovna is the elder sister in the Petrov family.

## Age

Thirty-two.

## Biography Facts

- She returned to the city six years ago after leaving medical school.
- She managed her father's household during his final illness.
- She does not trust religious explanations for the shadow phenomenon.
- She has a strained relationship with her younger brother Ilya.

## Personality

- restrained
- observant
- proud
- slow to forgive

## Canon Constraints

- Anna does not openly cry in public.
- She avoids direct confessions and tends to hide anger inside formal language.
- She was present at her father's funeral.
