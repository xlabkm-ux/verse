# Timeline: Book 1

## Ordered Events

1. Ilya Petrov returns to the city three days before the funeral.
2. The father dies after a long illness.
3. The funeral takes place on a cold overcast morning.
4. The first major argument between Anna and Ilya happens on the evening after the funeral.
5. After sunset that same evening, both siblings are still inside the old city.

## Hard Constraints

- The funeral happens before the sibling argument.
- The sibling argument in chapter 3 occurs after sunset.
- Any scene set during the argument must reflect the city's shadow rule.
