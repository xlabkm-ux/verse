# Chapter 03 Summary

Anna and Ilya leave the cemetery in silence after their father's funeral. By evening, resentment that had been contained during the ceremony begins to surface. Anna speaks with formal calm while Ilya hides behind wit and half-mocking apologies. Their argument escalates inside the old city after sunset, when the absence of shadows makes the street feel exposed and unreal.

## Canon Anchors

- the argument happens after the funeral, not before
- the tone is restrained at first, then escalating
- Anna avoids open displays of grief
- Ilya uses humor defensively
